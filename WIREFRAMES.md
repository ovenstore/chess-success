## home-regular

This is the home page for users who are not logged-in, there is a login and a register button in the nav bar, and there is no option to play the game. 

## home-logged-in

This is the home page for logged-in users, they are able to play games and log out. Note that in the real site the nav bar will display a log out button rather than the login and register buttons. 

## login

This is the login screen. 

## register

This is the screen to register an account, it prompts the user to confirm their password and email address. 

## leaderboard

This is the leaderboard, it will be viewable by anyone, whether they are signed-in or not. 

## game-play

This is what it will look like to play the game. The chess board is kind of bad in this render because the pieces don't contrast well with the board, this will be fixed in the actual game. 

## game-over

This is the screen that will show after a game is finished, it will show the player's total points with the modifier based on whether they won or lost. 

## game-review

This is the game review screen, it will allow the users to step through their past games move by move in order to review their gameplay.
